  • Cryptocurrency
01. How does Step Penny work?

Step Penny is a cryptocurrency that you can mine simply by moving around with our app running on your phone. The app tracks your movement, and for every step you take, you earn Step Penny coins.

02. Do I need any special equipment to mine Step Penny ?

Nope! All you need is your smartphone and the Step Penny app. Just download the app, turn on the mining feature, and start moving to earn coins.

03. Can I mine Step Penny while doing any activity?

Absolutely! Whether you're walking, jogging, cycling, or even just moving around your house, you can mine Step Penny and earn coins. The more you move, the more you earn..

04. How do I redeem the Step Penny coins I've earned?

Redeeming your Step Penny coins is easy. Simply go to the app's redemption section, select the amount of coins you want to redeem, and choose your preferred payout method, such as PayPal or bank transfer.

05. Is Step Penny safe and secure to use?

Yes, Step Penny is designed with security in mind. We use encryption to protect your data and transactions, and our platform is built on blockchain technology.

06. Can I transfer my Step Penny coins to other users?

Absolutely! You can transfer your Step Penny coins to other users within the app or even send them to external cryptocurrency wallets. It's quick, easy, and secure.

07. Are there any fee for mining or redeeming Step Penny?

Nope! We believe in keeping things simple and transparent. There are no mining fees, and when you redeem your Step Penny coins, you'll receive the full value without any hidden charges.

08. How often can I redeem my Step Penny coins?

There's no limit to how often you can redeem your Step Penny coins. You can redeem them as frequently as you like, whether it's daily, weekly, or monthly. It's entirely up to you!
